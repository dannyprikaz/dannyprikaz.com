About

Actor

Danny graduated from Oberlin College with High Honors in Theater.

Writer

Danny studied creative writing at Oberlin College where he focused on poetry. He has since refocused his writing ambitions onto screenplays. He is writing a web series with his writing partner, Dewey Cassady.

Improviser

Danny has been a member of the Los Angeles improv scene for two and a half years now. He has performed at UCB, iO West, Second City Hollywood, you name it. He's been on house teams at LA Connection Comedy, Second City, and The Improv Space. You can see him perform on any given Friday with his team, Hot for the Rent, at the Improv Space in Westwood at 8 PM.

Host

Danny hosts the online, streaming game show, Whodunnit League.
